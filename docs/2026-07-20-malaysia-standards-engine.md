# Malaysia Standards Engine — Phase 1

## Reviewed scope

The first standards record covers KSSM Form 1 Science, `5.1.3`: differentiating physical and chemical properties of matter. Its source evidence is a current KPM publication that identifies the Form 1, `5.1 Jirim dalam alam`, `5.1.3` learning standard.

## Safety boundary

The resolver is deliberately narrow. A generic `Jirim` or `States of matter` lesson does not receive the `5.1.3` label because it may cover different outcomes. Those requests remain teacher-review practice until a reviewed record is added.

## Output contract

For a reviewed match, the response carries `standards_context`, item-level `standard_id`, cognitive progression, evidence type, and `coverage_report`. The dashboard links the teacher to the reviewed KPM source.

## Source

- [KPM Laporan Analisis Item Sains TIMSS 2023](https://www.moe.gov.my/storage/files/shares/penerbitan_dan_jurnal/penyelidikan/pisa_timss_sea-plm/Laporan%20Analisis%20Item%20Sains%20TIMSS%202023.pdf)

## Platform expansion

The same registry now accepts independently reviewed country packs. The first cross-jurisdiction seeds are NGSS Grade 5 Science `5-PS1-1` and England Key Stage 3 Science particle-model matter. They are examples of the registry contract, not a claim of complete United States or United Kingdom coverage.
